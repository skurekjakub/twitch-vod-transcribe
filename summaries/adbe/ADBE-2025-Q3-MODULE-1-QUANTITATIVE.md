## Key Observations

1. **Beat and Raise Quarter:** Adobe exceeded Q3 targets and raised full-year revenue and EPS guidance
2. **AI Monetization Accelerating:** AI-influenced ARR surpassed $5B; AI-first ARR hit full-year target one quarter early
3. **Digital Media Inflection:** Net new ARR appears to be flat-to-growing QoQ for first time this year (per analyst question)
4. **Margin Resilience:** Operating margins holding above mid-40s despite AI investment cycle
5. **Enterprise Momentum:** Cross-cloud deals +60% YoY, demonstrating platform adoption
6. **Cash Generation:** Record Q3 operating cash flow of $2.20B
