**Nature:** A knowing, tongue-in-cheek comment acknowledging the chicken-and-egg question of whether Nvidia's hardware innovations enabled the deep learning revolution or whether Nvidia was responding to emerging demand from deep learning researchers. **Significance:** This brief aside touches on important questions about technological progress: Do hardware capabilities drive algorithmic innovation, or do algorithmic needs drive hardware development? The reality is likely co-evolutionary, with each advancing in response to the other.
