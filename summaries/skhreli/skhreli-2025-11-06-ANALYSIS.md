* **Duolingo has distribution advantage that OpenAI cannot easily replicate** - Despite AI capabilities, building engaging application with user retention is distinct skill. "OpenAI has unlimited capital" but "you need distribution."

* **Cell therapy mechanisms that sound primitive should be questioned** - Capricor's approach (crushing dead heart tissue and injecting) mocked as "what a prehistoric man would do."

* **Transparency matters in revenue reporting** - IonQ criticized for hiding organic growth vs. acquired revenue: "they keep hiding how much is coming from their business versus the businesses they bought."

* **Know the people behind the company** - Repeated references to knowing management teams personally (OpenDoor's Keith and Cass, Sam Altman/Elon Musk lawsuit dynamics).
